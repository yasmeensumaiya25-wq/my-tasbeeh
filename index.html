Guidance for Humanity

Surah Al-Baqarah · 2:286

لَا يُكَلِّفُ ٱللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا

"Allah does not burden a soul beyond that it can bear."

Surah Ar-Rahman · 55:13

فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

"So which of the favours of your Lord would you deny?"

Surah Al-Inshirah · 94:5-6

فَإِنَّ مَعَ ٱلْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا إِنَّ مَعَ ٱلْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا

"For indeed, with hardship comes ease. Indeed, with hardship comes ease."

Surah Al-Ankabut · 29:69

وَٱلَّذِينَ جَـٰهَدُوا۟ فِينَا لَنَهْدِيَنَّهُمْ سُبُلَنَا

"And those who strive for Us — We will surely guide them to Our ways."

Morning Dua

اللَّهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا

"O Allah, by Your grace we have reached the morning and by Your grace we reach the evening."

Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَعَلَى بَرَكَةِ اللَّهِ

"In the name of Allah and with the blessings of Allah."

Before Sleeping

بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

"In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live."

For Protection

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ

"In the name of Allah with whose name nothing can harm."

For Gratitude

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ

"O Allah, help me to remember You, thank You, and worship You well."

For Knowledge

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا
